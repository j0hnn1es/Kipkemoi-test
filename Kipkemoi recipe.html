Ugali Managu Beef Recipe

Ingridients:

For Beef

  • 500g beef cubes
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tbsp garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 3 tomatoes, diced
  • 4 cups of water
  • Coriander leaves, chopped

For Managu:

  • 500g African Nightshade leaves, chopped
  • 1 bunch of spinach leaves, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 6 tbsp cooking cream (optional)
  • 1 tbsp black Pepper, powdered
